Swimming Spots in London

London has many spots to go swim that I enjoy. There are many unique places around the city that offer an amazing experience.

  1. Parliament Hill Lido
  2. Hampstead swimming ponds
  3. Kentish Town Sports Centre
  4. London Fields Lido
  5. London Aquatics Centre

Parliament Hill Lido

Parliment Hill Lido is an unheated open air swimming pool located in North London. It is open to the public 365 days a year.

Hampstead Swimming Ponds

Hampstead has three ponds which are open to the public to swim in. There is a mens pond, a womens pond and a mixed pond. These are open air ponds where you can swim with the local wild life!

Kentish Town Sports Centre

Kentish Town Sports Centre has a historic pool which has been restored

London Fields Lido

London Fields Lido is a 50m Olympic size, heated outdoor swimming pool in Hackney.

London Aquatics Centre

London Aquatics Centre was used in the 2012 olympics and is now open to the public. It is located in Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park in Stratford.
